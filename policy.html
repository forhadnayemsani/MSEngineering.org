Policies

Religious and Ethical Policies

  • Honesty & Transparency: Every transaction and activity must uphold the highest standards of honesty. Deception or dishonesty is strictly prohibited.
  • Ethical & Halal Investments: The company will only invest in halal businesses and industries, avoiding any involvement in prohibited sectors.
  • Islamic Conduct: All employees must maintain ethical behavior, use respectful language, be punctual, and adhere to modesty guidelines.
  • Prayer Time Facilitation: Employees will be given dedicated time and space for performing their daily prayers.

Bussiness policies

  • Financial Discipline: Company resources and capital must be managed with transparency and proper planning.
  • Risk Management: Investments must be backed by thorough research and risk assessment.
  • Long-Term Vision: Short-term gains must not compromise the company’s long-term objectives.
  • Avoiding Greed: Trading and investments should be based on strategic analysis rather than emotional decisions or excessive greed.
  • Client & Investor Protection: All transactions must be transparent, prioritizing the best interests of clients and investors.

Employee & Leadership Policies

  • Merit-Based Recruitment & Promotion: Hiring and promotions will be based solely on skills, qualifications, and performance.
  • Teamwork & Mutual Respect: A collaborative work environment with mutual respect must be maintained.
  • Confidentiality: Company data and strategic plans must be protected and not shared without authorization.
  • Fair Compensation: Employees will be fairly compensated, and salaries will be paid on time.
  • Zero Tolerance for Injustice: Any form of mistreatment or discrimination against employees or clients will not be tolerated.
  • Employee Development: Training and growth opportunities will be provided to enhance employee skills.

Financial Policies

  • Accurate Accounting: All financial transactions must be properly documented and transparent.
  • Budget Planning: Financial planning will follow Islamic principles, ensuring no wasteful spending.
  • Profit Distribution: Profits will be distributed fairly, with reinvestments into the company’s growth and welfare initiatives.
  • Ethical Financing: All financial agreements must comply with Shariah law, and no earnings will come from haram sources.

Client Policies

  • Honesty & Transparency: No false or misleading information will be given to clients.
  • Fair Advice: Investors will receive fair and well-researched financial advice.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Complaints and concerns will be handled professionally and efficiently.
  • High-Quality Services: No compromise will be made on the quality of services and products offered.

Social Responsibility Policies

  • Charity & Community Support: The company will contribute to the welfare of the poor and underprivileged.
  • Environmental Protection: Business operations must not harm the environment.
  • Humanitarian & Justice-Oriented Approach: The company will uphold justice and ethical practices in all dealings.
  • Zakat & Donations: The company will fulfill its Zakat obligations and allocate funds for charitable causes.

Prohibited & Diciplinary Policies

  • No Haram Transactions: Interest-based dealings, bribery, gambling, and false advertising are strictly prohibited.
  • No Unethical Behavior: Corruption, indecency, or unethical conduct in the workplace will not be tolerated.
  • Disciplinary Actions: Any violation of company policies will result in appropriate disciplinary measures.

The Asset Architects Limited is committed to building a successful company that upholds Islamic values, professionalism, and integrity. Our goal is not just financial success but also the establishment of a fair and just economic system.
